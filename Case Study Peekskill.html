Managable Problem

There are not enough mentorship opportunities for young people in Peekskill

There are not enough mentorship opportunities for young people in Peekskill today, partly because there is not the funding to pay for someone to seek out and drive recruitment of mentors and mentees at the PYB.

This lack of opportunity leads to a loss of hope for some young people of Peekskill, who then turn to violence as means of having some sense of purpose.

If this problem continues to go unaddressed, the Healing Forward initiative will struggle to meet its goals of reducing teen violence in Peekskill.
